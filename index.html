Development of Container Receiving Prioritisation Model

I used Python and Statistics to make a combinatorial analysis of containers carrying various SKUs to decide receiving priority based on out-of-stock levels, warehouse usage and receiving capacity. The tool, after being applied, together with other strategies, reduced the average warehouse usage percentage in 10%, while the out-of-stock status of each SKU was reduced in up to 5 days.
